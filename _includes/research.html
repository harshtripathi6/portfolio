Integrating datasets with discrete and natural language annotations for person retrieval

This project was my first contribution to academia. The aim was to use soft biometric features for person identification in surveillance footage. I developed an NLP-based framework that mapped language prompts to a standardized format for the AVSS dataset, facilitating person retrieval in video. This work was published at the IoT/CPS-Security 2023 workshop at VTC 2023-Fall in Hong Kong.

Tools and Technologies: NLTK, SpaCy, Tkinter, Python, Roboflow
